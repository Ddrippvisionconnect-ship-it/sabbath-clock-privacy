Privacy Policy — Sabbath Clock

Last updated: November 7, 2025

Sabbath Clock is published by DDripp Vision Connect. We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Data used on your device

  • Location (When In Use): Used locally to compute sunset times. Not sent to any server.
  • Notifications: Local reminders for Shabbat times. You can change permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Local App Data: Notes/plans and display name are stored only on your device.

Third-party services

App uses Apple/Expo frameworks on device only; we operate no backend.

Your choices

  • Revoke Location/Notification permissions in Settings.
  • Delete the app to remove local data.

Children

We do not knowingly collect information from children.
